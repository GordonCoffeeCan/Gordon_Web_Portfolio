River Band and Bridge is a stylized envrionment design and is also 3D game art assets that are used in Rayha's Poem.
Objects are modeled in Maya, textured in Photoshop and Sbustance Painter, and Rendered in Sketchfab.

Dungeon Level Kit is 3D assets bundle that is used for level design in Rayha's Poem.
Objects are modeled in Maya and Zbrush. The texture are painted in Substance Painter and Photoshop.

Sailboat and Island is a 3D scene concept design in stylized texture.
The boat, island, coconut tree and pier are modeled in Maya and Zbrush, and baked Normal map for low-poly mesh.
Texture is painted in Substance Painter. The lightmap is baked in Unity and the scene is rendered by Sketchfab.

T-Rex is a 3D creature design. The T-Rex, rocks, wood and terrain are modeled in Zbrush, topoed in TopoGun and textured in Substance Painter.
All models in the scene are fully optimized and ready to use in the game engine.
